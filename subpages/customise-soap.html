Head note

Basically, each essential oil has a note, and that note helps determine how the oil will play with others. These notes can be separated into three categories: head, heart, and base. A head note is usually the first thing you`ll notice in a fragrance. They are usually fresh and uplifting and will also usually be the first to evaporate. Heart notes help give body and fullness to a blend. They`re often what holds the blend together and makes it work. Finally, we have base notes. It`s the foundation of your entire blend and will leave a lasting impression.
